About me :

I am a passionate biologist and a recent graduate of the BS-MS dual degree program at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Bhopal. My primary research training has been in the field of developmental biology in Drosophila melanogaster. My master's work focused on nucleoporins in Drosophila development.

Hailing from the ancient city of Varanasi, I follow a core belief shared by many in developmental biology: seeing is believing. This makes the confocal microscope my favorite instrument.
